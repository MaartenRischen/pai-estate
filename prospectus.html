The Property

A Perfectly Peaceful Paradise

Five minutes outside of Pai, in the serene village of Maenatung, surrounded by Northern Thailand's richest nature. A beautifully isolated gem with the most gorgeous direct sunset views and its own meandering river.

The gardens have been lovingly landscaped with a vast array of fruit trees and exotic flora, meditational spaces, and art pieces. There are two raised outdoor gazebos on the property.

All electricity and water systems were replaced and upgraded in 2024 with full three-phase electricity. Brand new concrete roads now run to and through the property. All houses have had regular maintenance and are in perfect liveable condition.
