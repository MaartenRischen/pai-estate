About the place

I bought this place for me and a group of friends — the idea was to grow old here together, make everything beautiful and functional. The group didn't work out, and it's too much for me to do on my own, so I'm selling.

Five minutes outside of Pai, in the quiet village of Maenatung. Very private, surrounded by nature, with direct sunset views and a river running through the land.

Lots of fruit trees and tropical plants throughout, plus meditation spots, art pieces, and two raised outdoor gazebos.

All electricity and water systems were replaced in 2024 with full three-phase electricity. New concrete roads run to and through the property. All houses are well maintained and liveable.
